Cheese burger

About the classic Cheese burger

Most of you have probably already had one before, but the cheese burger is a timeless staple of western society. It's simplicity in design, 2 buns, a slice of cheese and a burger makes it very accessible to beginner cookers. It's ability to be easily customisable with the toppings of your choice also makes it an extra attractive option.

Ingredients

  • 1KG of Minced beef
  • 300g of Breadcrumbs
  • 140g of cheddar cheese or sliced cheese
  • 1 bunch of small bunch parsley

Steps

  1. Crumble the mince in a bowl and then tip the breadcrumbs and cheese and then mix everything thoroughly.
  2. Shape the mix into 12 burgers and chill until ready to cook for up to 12 hours.
  3. Heat the grill to high and place the burgers on the grill. Let the burgers grill for 6-8 minutes.
  4. Toast the buns and wait for them to come out toasted.
  5. Assemble the burger and choose what toppings you would like.
