Fusuli Pasta

About Fusuli Pasta

A simple and personal favorite dish, fusuli pasta should be a staple in most peoples meal plans. With a suprising amount of protein, this dish is healthy for you and will do a good job in restoring your energy for people with active lifestyles. The versatile nature of this dish means that you can customise the dish with whatever toppings you may wish to do so. I personally like to use cheese and butter in my pasta.
